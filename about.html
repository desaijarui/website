About

Jarui Desai is a passionate business professional, entrepreneur, and social innovator. Hailing from a family of entrepreneurs, her motivation stems from her constant desire to learn, create, cultivate, enhance, and thrive.

Currently, she is the Co-Founder and Executive Director of Operations at Droplet Water Project, a non-profit organization focused on improving the global effort of providing access to clean drinking water in a unique way. Apart from leading the business side of Droplet, Jarui also works as the Head of Logistics for V.Tiers, an organization committed to making an eternal impact on communities around the world. Furthermore, she is a Client Success Specialist at WellAware, an Industrial IoT company that empowers organizations to be efficient, safe, and sustainable by streaming mission-critical data to their employees, enabling them to remotely monitor and control physical assets. Learn more about her leadership roles here.

Within the last six years, gaining experience working at global corporations (Fortune 100 and 500), mid-sized companies, start-ups, and not-for-profit organizations, Jarui has demonstrated competency in areas such as accounting, finance, marketing, customer success, and information technology. As a result, she has had the honor of being recognized as a 2019 Venture for America Fellow and a Forbes Under 30 Scholar. She hopes to continue incorporating innovative practices and techniques in order to ensure future business success.

Jarui is also a strong proponent of paying-it-forward. She realized her passion for service through Girl Scouts, and has made an effort to stay engaged with her community by giving back. 17 years, 14 service-based organizations, numerous hours, and countless memories later, she continues to push for a self-sufficient world.
